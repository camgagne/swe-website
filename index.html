Project Description

Endure and Survive is an educational survival game that teaches players real-world outdoor skills in a fun, interactive way. Designed for middle-school aged kids, the game encourages problem solving and creativity with activities and puzzles relating to real world survival skills such as fire making and navigation. The game teaches presents each of these challenges through a series of levels, each with an objective tailored to a specific real world skill.

Background & Motivation

Educational games that make learning more interesting for younger audiences are becoming more and more popular. These "edutainment" games give children the chance to master skills like basic survival and navigation that they might not otherwise be exposed to. Endure and Survive was developed to help close that gap by teaching students survival skills in a safe and secure setting in an entertaining and engaging manner. The project is motivated by the fact that children learn best by doing, regardless if they succeed or fail. Our objective is to develop a game that teaches young students valuable survival skills, by creating a memorable and long-lasting educational experience.
